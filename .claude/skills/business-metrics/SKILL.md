# SKILL FILE: skill-business-metrics.md
# Loaded by agent when: analysing business performance, conversion, growth opportunities
# Purpose: Full business metrics audit — conversion, revenue, retention, market, AI opportunities

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Takes a business (website, screenshot, description, or brief) and runs a
complete business metrics analysis. Covers conversion opportunities,
revenue metrics, retention, market positioning, competitive gaps,
UX-to-revenue connections, and AI enhancement possibilities.
Always connects design decisions to business outcomes.

---

## STEP 3 — REVENUE METRICS ANALYSIS

### 3A — Revenue Levers
Identify every revenue lever available to this business:

PRIMARY LEVERS:
1. Average order value (AOV) — can the site upsell or cross-sell?
2. Purchase frequency — can the site drive repeat customers?
3. New customer acquisition — is the site findable and compelling?
4. Conversion rate — what % of visitors take action?
5. Customer lifetime value (CLV) — is there a loyalty mechanism?

For each lever, identify:
- Current state on the site
- Opportunity (what's missing)
- Estimated revenue impact (Low / Medium / High / Very High)
- Effort to implement (Quick win / Medium / Complex)

### 3B — Revenue Opportunity Matrix

| Opportunity | Revenue impact | Effort | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Specific opportunity] | High/Med/Low | Low/Med/High | P1/P2/P3 |

Sort by: High impact + Low effort = P1 (do first)

### 3C — Pricing and AOV Strategy
- Is pricing visible? If hidden, estimate % of users who leave without knowing
- Are upsell opportunities present? (Add-ons, bundles, upgrades)
- Are there occasion-based premium options? (Wedding, corporate, sympathy)
- Is there a minimum order value communicated?
- Recommendation: how to increase AOV by 20–40% through UX alone

## STEP 7 — AI ENHANCEMENT OPPORTUNITIES

Note: AI is not always the answer. Only recommend where it genuinely adds value.
Flag with: STRONG FIT / POSSIBLE FIT / NOT NEEDED

### AI opportunities to evaluate per business:

STRONG FIT — high ROI, proven technology:
- AI chat widget for after-hours enquiries (converts leads that would otherwise be lost)
- AI-powered personalisation (show relevant products based on occasion or history)
- Automated review response (respond to Google/Yelp reviews consistently)
- Smart email sequences (birthday/anniversary reminders triggered by purchase history)

POSSIBLE FIT — worth exploring:
- AI image search (find flowers by uploading a photo)
- Predictive inventory for seasonal demand
- Dynamic pricing for peak seasons (Valentine's, Mother's Day)
- AI-generated arrangement descriptions for SEO

NOT NEEDED — where AI adds complexity without clear ROI:
- AI chatbots that replace a simple phone call for a local shop
- Complex recommendation engines for small catalogues
- AI video generation for a trust-based local business

For each AI opportunity:
OPPORTUNITY: [name]
Fit: STRONG / POSSIBLE / NOT NEEDED
Why: [one line reason]
Revenue connection: [which metric it improves]
Implementation complexity: Low / Medium / High
Estimated impact: Low / Medium / High / Very High
